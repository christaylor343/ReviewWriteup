Forza Horizon 4 is probably the best arcade racing game on the market. I have had endless amounts of fun
racing, drifting, and checking off all the boxes on completing the activities. The whole time I 've had a blast
collecting and modifying dozens of cars. They make it so easy to find or make a tuning, find a paintjob,
and make the vehicle your own. There is even a mechanic to level up the amount of experience and credits
you get with your favorite cars.


I had the pleasure of viewing the game on an Xbox One X and a nicely powered gaming PC and it is simply incredible.
4k and HDR make the game pop and is a showpiece.

The online functionality is seamless and well-integrated. Other players will zip by on their way to their own races and challenges
and you can stop at anytime and do a 1 on 1 race, anytime, anywhere. If you're like me, I mostly play offline,
and the offline CPU racers actually mimic other people on your friends list using their Drivatars, which is constantly learning.


Overall, I think that the racing experience Horizon 4 provides speaks for itself and for right now, is the bar for which,
I would measure other racing games by. The amount of constantly updating content, the player made customizations,
and versatility of being anything from serious track-racing, to extreme off-road motorsports.

Final Verdict: 5/5
